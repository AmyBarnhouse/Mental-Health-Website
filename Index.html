Dealing with the Stigma

Many people remain silent when it comes to tackling mental health problems. Through speaking out and sharing experiences, others can gain the confidence to contribute.

Try to connect yourself with friends to prevent feeling isolated. There are many treatments available to you, delivered in different ways to suit preferences.
